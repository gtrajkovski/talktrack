## Description (260 words max)

**The Problem:**
You have a big presentation tomorrow. You want to rehearse in your car on the commute. But every practice app requires looking at a screen — impossible while driving.

**The Solution:**
TalkTrack is the first rehearsal tool designed for commuters. Import your presentation, put your phone down, and practice entirely by voice.

**How It Works:**
1. **Import** — Paste notes, upload PPTX/PDF, or read from a URL
2. **Listen** — Hear your slides read aloud to refresh your memory
3. **Prompt** — Hear just the title, then recite from memory
4. **Test** — Pure recall mode with scoring and feedback

**All Voice-Controlled:**
Say "next" to advance. Say "reveal" if you're stuck. Say "repeat" to hear it again. Say "help" for coaching. No tapping required.

**Features:**
- 3 rehearsal modes (Listen, Prompt, Test)
- 35+ voice commands in 6 languages
- AI coaching feedback after sessions
- Sentence/paragraph/slide granularity
- Spaced repetition for weak slides
- CarPlay/Android Auto dashboard integration
- Works offline as a PWA

**Who It's For:**
- Conference speakers on the road
- Sales reps preparing pitches
- Students memorizing presentations
- Pastors rehearsing sermons
- Anyone who commutes

Try the web app free at talktrack.app, or install from Google Play.

## Maker Story (for About section)

Built by a conference speaker who got tired of reading notes while driving.

The problem seemed simple: practice my talk in the car. But every app — PowerPoint, Speeko, Orai — required looking at a screen. Speech coaches say rehearse out loud, but how do you rehearse when you can't see your notes?

TalkTrack started as a weekend project and became an obsession: what if the entire app worked by voice? What if you could complete a full rehearsal session without touching your phone once?

The result is the first truly eyes-free presentation practice tool. Your phone stays in your pocket. You just talk.
